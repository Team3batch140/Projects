Vietnam seal on the bridge connecting the shore

Not only is the important works help transportation more convenient, many bridges in Vietnam also have unique architecture, bearing the mark of history and time.

The beautiful, especially meaningful bridge is quite popular topic selected readers in the contest "Imprint Vietnam". The photo of Nhat Tan Bridge (Hanoi) in the first week is equipped with new lighting system. The bridge across the Red River, connecting West Lake with Dong Anh District, is the longest cable-stayed bridge in Vietnam and is said to be the new symbol, the pride of the modern capital. 5 spans of cushion symbolize the five cellular doors.

Referring to the bridge can not be ignored Da Nang - where is known as the city of the bridge, prominent among them is the Dragon Bridge. With architecture simulating a dragon flying over the Han River, Dragon Bridge is recognized as one of the most unique and innovative bridge designs in Vietnam, possessing many international awards.

The Nguyen Van Troi bridge is the oldest of the six bridges connecting the banks of the Han River, built by the US in 1965 to serve the war. After more than half a century of existence, the bridge was seriously degraded. Now the bridge serves as a souvenir of Danang, becoming a walking street and developing tourism. Picture: Pham Doan Trieu.

Sunset moment falls on My Loi Bridge - an important bridge connecting two provinces of Long An - Tien Giang. Located on the National Highway 50, the bridge is 2.6 km long, across the Vam Co River helps significantly shorten the distance from Ho Chi Minh City to the provinces in the Mekong Delta, while playing an important role. in the cause of economic development and national defense security

Can Tho Bridge connects the banks of the Hau River as well as the important transportation route of Can Tho City and the longest main span of Southeast Asia (550 m) at the inauguration (2010). This is considered to contribute greatly to the socio-economic development of the Mekong Delta.

Master of Monk Huyen Son Son Thuong step over Truong Tien bridge on the way begging. Located in the middle of Hue City, this is the first bridge over the romantic Perfume River, designed in the Gothic style, undergoing many restorations in history and appeared in many Vietnamese poetry works. This is also a symbol of Hue city's attractions.

Bai Chay Bridge (Quang Ninh) is the first cable-stayed bridge in Vietnam, strategically located in transportation, economy and defense. This is also one of the attractions when visiting the city of Halong.

With over 100 years of existence, Long Bien Bridge has been dubbed the historic witness of Hanoi capital when the people experienced two long-term resistance of the nation, containing many cultural and historical values. This is the first steel bridge across the Red River, connecting two districts of Hoan Kiem and Long Bien.

Some new bridges are on the way to completion such as Nhat Le 2 (Quang Binh). This is defined as key projects, economic promotion, tourism development and improve the image of Dong Hoi city.
